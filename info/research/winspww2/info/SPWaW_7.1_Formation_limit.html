I think the code was changed back around v4 to allow no more than 200 formations and 400 units total for both sides added together.

If you are buying formations with an average of 4 units each (platoons) you are pegging out the max unit limit before the max formation limit.

Try buying snipers, you should be able to buy more than 100.

The game becomes more unstable with more units, if you put all possible units out there, you tend to lose crews when vehicles are destroyed, etc.
thanks, John.

The good news is that if all the units of a formation are killed it opens up a slot to buy reinforcements. I was just begging my opponent to kill my crewmen in one battle.

Try not to get too close to the limits though for better gameplay.
